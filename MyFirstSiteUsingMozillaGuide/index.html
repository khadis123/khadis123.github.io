Is Bitcoin money or not?

The answer is yes and no. Don't worry. I'll explain you what it means.

In a nutshell, bitcoin is money, but the biggest difference is it has no central authority controlling it like FRS and the exact amount of money to be coined / issued is known in advance to all. Actually, it's been programmatically defined by the Bitcoin creator called Satoshi Nakamoto (this is his nickname).

There're 5 main functions of money:

  • Medium of exchange
  • Measure of value
  • Standard of deferred payment
  • Store of value
  • International money

There're some variations of the functions given above but you got the bigger picture and it's enough for now. In case if you want to know more, read this article.
